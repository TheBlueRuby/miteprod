Who Are We?

Mite Productions is a small game development group based out of Australia.

We aim to make fun games for people to play, and help people learn to make some themselves.


What do we do?

We make games for people to play, and open-source them so people can learn from them.

We are doing this to thank the game industry, and to learn and help others learn programming.
